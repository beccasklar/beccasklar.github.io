About Me

Hello everyone!

My name is Becca Sklar and I am a sophomore at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I am majoring in Journalism on the Strategic Communications track with a certificate in Digital Studies. I am originally from Scarsdale, NY and I absolutely love living in Madison.

I’m a super creative person, and I love working with my peers and sharing my ideas. I really want to go into communications because I’m very social and want an exuberant, collaborative workspace. I love to write and create, and I hope to utilize new technology to make digital content that will inform and inspire others. I have been enthusiastic about journalism since I was in high school, and I have had the pleasure of working with a number of magazines, newspapers, and online blogs that have allowed me to build important skills and get involved with the field of journalistic writing and digital creation. I have also become very interested in graphic design, and I hope to incorporate the skills I am learning in this field into my career in the future.

In terms of extracurriculars, I have done theatre since I was very young. Aside from being inolved in a number of productions as I grew up, I also worked for my studio as an assistant director and helped put on a number of stage musicals near my hometown. I also am Jewish, which is something that has always been important to me. I was on the board of my chapter of my Jewish youth group, and I remained heavily involved with my synagogue throughout my years in high school. When I came to campus, I discovered a number of Jewish fellowships, such as Hillel and Chabad, where I have been able to connect with other Jews in Madison and engage with services and other fun programs. I also recently spent my summer in Israel, which was an incredibly special experience. Lastly, I am involved with Greek Life at UW-Madison, and it has been so much fun to make friends with so many new girls from all around the country. I am a part of Chi Omega on campus, and we have done a lot of amazing work for our national philanthropy, Make-A-Wish, through large fundraisers and events. I have been an active member for 2 years, and it has been an incredible part of my life here in Madison.

When I'm not in school, I enjoy lots of different fun hobbies. Some of my interests include hanging out with my friends, listening to music, watching movies, dogs, travel, being a foodie, and doing my makeup. I love meeting new people, trying new things, and having an open mind. A fun fact about me is that I have been vegetarian for 13 years! I have loved my time at UW-Madison, and some of my favorite things here are gamedays and the food on campus. My favorite restaurant in Madison has to be Ha Long Bay (check out their website here). I'm a super adventerous eater and I love Thai food! I'm extremely fun-loving and curious, and I'm looking forward to gaining new opportunities throughout college and in life as a whole.
